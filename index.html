I AM

VAHBIZ DORABJEE

It was the October of 2010 when television opened gates to an eager soul wanting to make it large on the Indian small screen. Debuting with Pyaar Kii Yeh Ek Kahaani, in no time Vahbiz Dorabjee’s character “Panchi” soar to fame and was a household name. She later won the award for same buzzing character for the best supporting role in 2011 for Pyaar Kii Ye Ek Kahaani.

After surfacing on the small screen for 7 years, in October 2017 we witnessed the woman, credited with much fame and love, launching her all-new chat show “Showbiz With Vahbiz”.


As star-studded as the launch was, the show basked in paparazzi. Considering the reputation of Vahbiz in the industry and some major bonds the woman has also been able to create a impressive peer set.


Vahbiz almost effortlessly made a parallel career out of the flourishing event industry.


In the year of 2013, Vahbiz played the role of Alak in the television series Saraswatichandra.


She was also part of the television series Bahu Hamari Rajni Kant where she played the role of Maggie Kant.


She was the show stopper for Lakme Fashion week in 2019 for the designer Rina Dhaka with a social message on body shaming, trying to give wings to plus size women.


In 2019 again, she played a role in the web series Kanshmakash on Hungama Play.


Some of her major acheivements:

Social Influencer various top brands Urban clap, Denial, Muffti , Charles & Keith, Aldo, Promod, Havmor ice cream , Ranilal 1988 jewellers Many more.

Travel and lifestyle blogger.
